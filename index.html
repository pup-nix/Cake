Ingredients

Cake Ingredients

  • Devils Food Cake Mix
  • 1-1/4 cups Water
  • 1/2 cup Vegetable Oil
  • 3 Eggs

Whipped Topping

  • 2 packs Dr Oetker Whip it
  • 2 cups Heavy Whhipping Cream

Topping

  • 1 can Cherry Pie Filling
  • Chocolate Bark

Directions

Cake Instructions

1.Heat oven to 350F degrees for shiny metal or glass pan or 325 F for nonstick pan. Grease bottom only of 9" round pan or bottom and sides of all other pans.

2.Mix Cake mix, water, oil, and eggs in large bowl with mixer on medium speed or beat vigorously byt hand 2 minutes. Pour into pan.

3.Bake for 25-30 minutes or until toothpick in center comes out clean. Cool 10 minutes before removing from pan.

4.Wrap cake in plastic wrap ater cooled and freeze for 30 minutes.

Whipped Topping Instruction

1.Simply add contents of both packs of Whip it to 2 cups cold heavy whipping cream and beat until peaks form.

Instructions to put it all together.

1.Pull cake out of the freezer, unwrap, and place on a cake platform.

2.Spread whipped topping on the and around the cake. Decorate with remaining whipped topping.

3.Spread cherry topping on top of the whipped topping.

4.Garnish with shaved baking chocolate.
